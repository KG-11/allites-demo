About Us

A Special care for your healthy living

Our company was incorporated as Allites Life Sciences Private Limited on April 18, 2017 as a private limited company under the company act 2013. Our name embodies our purpose and belief of the entire company which is to give Elite Care ( ALLITES ) to patients for enabling them to lead healthier lives. Allites was crafted out of passion in Chennai by a group of extensively experienced pharmaceuticals professionals. From the day one in Aliites , we are maintaining everything in International standards. Allites Life Sciences is an ISO 9001-2015 certified company..
